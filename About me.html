About me

👋 Hello there!

Nice to see you here! My name is Yang Chen and I am a product/UX designer based in the Netherlands. Originally from Inner Mongolia, China, I came to the Netherlands in 2013 for my Master study in Media Technology. Currently I am living in the city of Utrecht, together with my husband and our two cats - Mochi and Lambiek (they are the best cats ever).

⛵ My journey to UX Design

I have always known that I am attracted to certain things: interactivity, technology, highly visual things, psychology...I decided to become a UX designer thanks to a book - "The Design of Everyday Things" by Don Norman. From this book, I learned about the power of human-centered design and started to look at the world from a completely different angle. This book introduced me to user experience design - and that was my "Eureka moment". I immersed myself into the field of UX - I took lessons, read books and articles, constantly practicing my skills and learning everything I could about UX design.

🐾 Work experience

I started working as a UX designer in 2016, first at Studyportals - a company based in Eindhoven, where I worked as a UX designer, designing for a series of SP's client-facing products and later also student-facing platform: Mastersportal.com.

Later, I joined IDEXX Animana as a UX designer, responsible for all the design & research activities for Animana (information management system for veterinary practices). Currently, I am working remotely with another IDEXX team who's building communication tools for veterinary clinics, and at the same time helping out with product discovery activities for pet owner facing products.

Along the way, I had the luck of meeting and working with some great people from whom I learned a great deal.

💃 Outside of working hours

When I am not designing, I am usually reading, spending time with my cats, learning Dutch, drumming, making pop-up cards, playing Redecor (well, still designing!), gardening, or having a "weekendje weg" with my husband...Covid-19 has forced us to spend most of the time at home but I always find ways to keep myself busy!

If you are looking for a UX designer, or need UX advice, or simply looking for someone to chat about UX, let's get in touch!
